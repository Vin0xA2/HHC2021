In-game terminal
Objective: Chomp all the True statements. Complete a stage in Potpourri at Intermediate or higher to get a special achievement!
References:
  http://www.natna.info/English/Teaching/CSI30-materials/Chapter1-cheat-sheet.pdf
  http://notes.imt-decal.org/sets/cheat-sheet.html

Controls:
  Arrow keys or WASD: Move Chompy
  Enter or Space: Chomp the current expression
  Click: Navigate to adjacent squares and chomp the square Chompy's in
  Esc: Pause the game
  i: Toggle text and iconography

Lessons learned:
  A << B, left shifts A by B bits.
  A >> B, right shifts A by B bits.
